What is
Muslin?

  • Lightweight, plain-woven cotton fabric with a soft open weave
  • Originally crafted in Dhaka (Bangladesh) & Mosul (Iraq)
  • Available in bleached, unbleached & dyed varieties
  • Centuries old — and still loved today
  • Gets softer with every single wash 🌿
